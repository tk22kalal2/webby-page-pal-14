Air India Dreamliner Crash at BJ Medical College

June 12, 2025 Ahmedabad

Air India flight crashes into BJ Medical College hostel, highlighting safety risks for medical students...

Incident Details

An Air India flight (AI171) bound for London crashed into the hostel of BJ Medical College in Ahmedabad shortly after takeoff, killing 241 onboard and 19 on the ground. The crash destroyed part of the medical college hostel, injuring dozens of students.

Impact on Medical Education

The sole survivor was a passenger seated near an emergency exit. The incident highlighted safety risks for medical students, as the hostel was unprepared for such a disaster. DNA identification of victims continued for weeks.

R.G. Kar Medical College Case

August 9, 2024 - Ongoing Kolkata

Trainee doctor case leads to nationwide protests and Supreme Court intervention...

Case Background

A trainee doctor was raped and murdered on campus, leading to nationwide protests. The Supreme Court took suo moto cognizance, forming a task force for doctor safety protocols.

Legal Proceedings

The CBI clarified it was not a gang rape but continued probing evidence destruction and cover-up allegations. The accused, a former civic police volunteer, was sentenced to life imprisonment in January 2025, but protests over unsafe conditions persisted.

High Priority

Telangana Medical College Expansion Crisis

June 27, 2025 Telangana

Rapid expansion from 5 to 34 colleges leaves many facilities understaffed and dysfunctional...

Expansion Issues

Telangana's rapid expansion of government medical colleges (from 5 to 34 since 2014) left many facilities understaffed and dysfunctional. Students reported overcrowded hostels, unpaid faculty, and lack of basic infrastructure.

Regulatory Response

The NMC issued show-cause notices to 26 colleges. Key issues include unopened hospitals (e.g., Siddipet's 1,000-bed hospital remains unused since 2023) and faculty shortages (1,736 out of 3,143 assistant professor posts vacant).

Infrastructure Crisis

Senior Resident Doctor Suicide

March 26, 2024 Kerala

Mental health crisis among resident doctors highlighted by tragic incident...

Incident Details

Dr. Abhirami Balakrishnan, a senior resident at Thiruvananthapuram Government Medical College, was found dead in her flat from a suspected IV drug overdose.

Systemic Issues

Police cited personal struggles but noted systemic stress among resident doctors. This incident highlights the urgent need for mental health support systems in medical education institutions.

Mental Health Crisis

Tamil Nadu Medical College Staffing Crisis

Recent Tamil Nadu

Severe faculty shortages affect quality of medical education across the state...

Staffing Crisis

Tamil Nadu's health minister dismissed NMC notices about vacancies in 35/36 government medical colleges as "routine," but doctors' associations revealed 700+ unfilled posts, including 400 assistant professors.

Root Causes

The crisis stems from delayed promotions and ongoing litigation. This shortage directly impacts the quality of medical education and patient care in government hospitals.
